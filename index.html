Nuclear Energy: Is the Risk Worth the Reward?

A Visual Perspective

Different Agencies and Hedge Funds have different metrics to calculate Levelized Cost of Energy (LCOE). Per this chart, Nuclear Energy is on similar cost effectiveness to Coal, Wind, Gas and Geothermal, while being one of the most clean energy sources available.

Carbon Dioxide Emissions of different fuel technologies

From the graph, it is clear that Nuclear Energy is among the cleanest sources of electricity generation with lowest minimum of 3.7 g CO2 equivalent/kWh and a median of 12 g CO2 equivalent/kWh.

Deaths per TWh of energy caused by different fuel technologies including accidents and air pollution

Cleanest energy sources also prove to be the safest energy sources. Nuclear Energy along with other renewables cause a small fraction of Deaths, which are insignificant when compared to Deaths caused by Coal and Natural Gas.
